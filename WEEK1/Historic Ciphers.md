# Historic Ciphers
We now enter the domain of Encryption and Decryption. Think of a situation - You want to securely send a msg to your friend? how would you do it, can even information ever be hidden even without storing it/physically hiding it?
Well, these were the ancient philosophers ideas, Julius caeser was one of them, idk he proposed or was used by him but the cipher we are going to talk about is the caeser cipher
It was just a +n version of the letter list, hence it could be cracked easily, then came the idea of substitution cipher - taking all permuations of the msg.
How to crack it? here comes the idea frrom the fact that some letters occur more and others less, taking this into consideration we use the frequency analysis to break it.
